Who We Are

A small studio with a
clear philosophy.

We are building Team 21 Studios around four commitments: clarity in gameplay, respect for player time, disciplined execution, and long-term quality.

🎮

Design for Decisions

Encounters are built around meaningful choices, not noise. Players should always understand why they won or lost.

🎨

Build for Readability

Visual style is not just aesthetic. It must support combat clarity, quick recognition, and strong moment-to-moment feedback.

⚙️

Execute Relentlessly

We prototype quickly, test honestly, and polish hard. Craft quality comes from iteration, not assumptions.

🔁

Earn Replayability

Depth is intentional. Builds, enemy patterns, and progression are tuned to reward mastery across repeated runs.
